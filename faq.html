    What to do if there is a buffering or freezing problem during streaming?

    An unstable internet connection is the main responsibility for all the streaming problems. For this we usually recommend our customers to use Ethernet cable for streaming. Restart your device if you are still lagging. You can check if your device is managing RAM and ROM streaming. The problem is quite rare from our server end and even if such things happen we guarantee you to repair the connections quickly. You can contact us and create tickets for your problems so we can fix them in a moment.

    Is it necessary to turn off the modem/device when not in use?

    You do not need to switch your device when not in use, but it does help you save electricity and reduce your Internet load. Many websites and applications consume data, and its important to turn off WiFi on your device even when youre not using them. Another advantage is that hackers cannot target your device when it is switched on. So its best to keep your device turned off when not in use.
